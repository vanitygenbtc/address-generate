Import a Private Key into blockchain.com

Using Blockchain Wallet browser version only, open Setting > Addresses.
Then click Import Existing Bitcoin Address.
You can enter WIF (Wallet Import/Export Format) or compressed private key.
Also you can scan QR code keys from paper wallet. This will allow you to actually send and receive bitcoins to and from this bitcoin address.

Site may propose you to transfer all bitcoins from imported address to integrated HD wallet. This is for your security.
But you can keep imported keys with new address into wallet, without transfer.

How to import WIF into blockchain.info wallet
(Step by step)

  1. log into your blockchain.info account
  2. click SETTINGS
  3. click Addresses
  4. click + Import Bitcoin Address
  5. click Import Address button
  6. paste your private key
  7. click Import
